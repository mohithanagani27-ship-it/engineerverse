What is Torque?

Torque is the rotational force applied to an object. It is widely used in mechanical engineering, machine design, automobile engineering, robotics and industrial systems.

Torque Formula

Torque = Force × Distance

T = F × r

SI Unit of Torque

The SI unit of torque is Newton-meter (Nm).

Applications of Torque

  • Automobile engines
  • Gear systems
  • Industrial machines
  • Robotics
  • Mechanical power transmission

Example Calculation

If force = 10 N and distance = 2 m

Torque = 10 × 2 = 20 Nm
